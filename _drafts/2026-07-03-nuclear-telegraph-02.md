### Why This Works Better

* **No Throat-Clearing:** We go straight to the throat in the first sentence.
* **The Two Questions:** By placing *"Are we a country..."* and *"What would it make us..."* right up front, you force the reader to answer them in their own mind before they even read your solution.
* **The Engineering Fix:** Repurposing the subs to hold sea drones and conventional munitions changes the conclusion from a passive "just get rid of them" to an active "let's build a better tool." It’s cleaner, sharper, and completely un-patronizing.
