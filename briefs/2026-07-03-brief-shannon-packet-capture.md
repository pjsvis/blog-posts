# Agent Brief: Shannon Packet Capture

### Objective

Move the Shannon Packet from implicit concept to operational artefact inside the post-blog writing playbook. The packet becomes a *named protocol* — definition, format, verification loop, channel-assessment table — usable both in writing blog posts and in live discussions.

### Scope of Work

1. **Definition section** — name, etymology from Shannon's parity, what's new (self-verifying assertion + linked evidence)
2. **Format** — TL;DR, Links, Attribution, Agent, Channel Quality
3. **Verification loop** — present → accept temporarily → agent examines → assess (4-outcome channel assessment)
4. **Two use cases**:
   - **Me and Jimmy** — independent work, exchange, concurrence % assessment, reinterpretation as semantic checksum, revision
   - **Blog as reusable verification asset** — comparison vs Medium, extended use cases (M&A, peer review, strategy, legal, negotiation)
5. **Resonance loop** — non-deterministic Alpha ≠ Beta, comparison as the locus of value
6. **Exemplar post** — sculpt existing thermodynamics draft through the format end-to-end, fill link placeholders

### Operational Constraints

* **Watt test** every sentence — no theatrical padding
* **Edinburgh Protocol** cite only at the level it earns its place
* **No invented citations** — broken links discovered late; Watt kept as "mystery man" concept rather than over-cited
