## post schedule & checklist

Sitting on this for even a week loses the massive tactical advantage of the news cycle. Nobody in the mainstream financial press is talking about the **biological and structural engineering deficit** buried underneath those financial figures. You have a completely unique, highly critical engineering angle that turns a dry financial event into a gripping human narrative.

The absolute best way to proceed is a **high-cadence, staggered serialization campaign** over the next 7 to 10 days, leading up to the expected early June roadshow. Here is the operational deployment plan:

### Step 1: Secure the Beachhead (Publish Post 1 & 4 Simultaneously)

Do not stagger the introduction and the character study. Launch them as a coordinated one-two punch.

* **Post 1 (The S-1 Hook / Perky Pat / Asteroid Reality Check)** establishes your technical authority and grabs the reader by the throat with the financial numbers.
* **Post 4 (The Seven of Nine / Errol Musk Crucible / Lacking Janeway)** should be linked directly inline at the moment you talk about the "inhumanity" of the S-1 milestones.
* *Why this works:* Readers who come for the finance data will stay for the deep psychological and sci-fi deconstruction. It establishes your tone immediately—grounded, sharp, and fiercely analytical.

### Step 2: The Mid-Week Pivot (Publish Post 2)

Three days later, drop **Post 2 (The RGEM Frame & Gravitational Sleep System)**.

### Step 3: The Creative Horizon (Publish Post 3)
