# Brief / Debrief

The primary process artefacts for requirements and work management in the silo.

## Brief

Written before work begins. Describes what will be done and why.

**Use for:**
- Proposing new posts, features, or system changes
- Capturing the objective, content outline, and constraints before drafting
- Establishing what conditions must already exist for the work to proceed

**Location:** `briefs/brief-[topic]-[date].md`

## Debrief

Written after work is completed or abandoned. Describes what happened and what was decided.

**Use for:**
- Recording outcomes, decisions made, and reasoning behind them
- Capturing what was learned that might inform future work
- Documenting the state of the system at the end of the work cycle

**Location:** `debriefs/[date]-[topic].md`

## Epic

A brief that groups related posts or deliverables. Used for multi-post series or multi-feature projects.

**Location:** `briefs/epic-[name].md`

## Relationship to Triage

Briefs are the input to the triage process. The agent surfaces requirements through the brief. Human personnel perform triage — categorizing each requirement as Enable, Strengthen, or Enhance — and document decisions in the debrief.

Without a brief, the agent surface is not structured. Without a debrief, triage decisions are not recorded. Both are required for the process to function.
