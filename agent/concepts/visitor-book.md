# Visitor's Book

The persistent record of external observations about the silo. It is not a brief. It does not require action.

It is a documented perspective — available to all silo participants, acted upon by developers, or ignored. The correct epistemic weight for a visitor's observation: *I observed this; you decide.*

**Location:** `visitors/BOOK.md`

**Format:**
```markdown
## Entry — YYYY-MM-DD

**Observation scope:** [what was examined]

### Observations
[Documented observations]

### Recommendations (Optional)
[Noted — no obligation to act]
```

Entries are additive. They are not deleted. A visitor's observation that is not acted upon remains in the record — it may be revisited, or it may stand as evidence of a gap that was known and not addressed.

The visitor's book is the artefact produced by visitor-mode agents. Signing it is the handoff requirement for visitor mode.
