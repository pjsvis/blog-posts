# Visitor's Book

The visitor's book is the persistent record of external observations about this silo. It is not a brief. It does not require action. It is a documented perspective available to all silo participants.

Entries are additive. They are not deleted. An observation that is not acted upon remains in the record.

---

*Format for new entries:*

```
## Entry — YYYY-MM-DD

**Observation scope:** [what was examined]

### Observations
[Documented observations]

### Recommendations (Optional)
[Noted — no obligation to act]
```
